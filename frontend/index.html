üöÄ Two Ways to Use with Seestar S50:

Option 1: Direct Control (Recommended - Built into this app!)
1. Generate your observing plan above
2. Go to the Observe tab
3. Enter your Seestar's IP address (default: 192.168.2.47) and click Connect
4. Click Execute Plan to start fully automated imaging
5. Watch real-time progress as your Seestar captures each target
‚ú® No external software needed! This app connects directly to your Seestar S50.

Option 2: Export for seestar_alp
1. Click "seestar_alp CSV" to download your observing plan
2. Set up seestar_alp on a Raspberry Pi or computer
3. Connect your Seestar S50 to WiFi (Station Mode)
4. Import the CSV file in seestar_alp web interface and start imaging
Note: The official Seestar app has no import capability. seestar_alp is the community standard.

‚ö†Ô∏è Your Mileage May Vary
These preview images are professional captures or survey data. Your actual images will vary based on:
  • Light pollution - Bortle scale at your location greatly affects results
  • Equipment - Different telescopes and cameras capture different detail
  • Exposure time - Longer exposures reveal more detail
  • Processing - Post-processing techniques dramatically affect final images
  • Atmospheric conditions - Seeing, transparency, and weather impact quality
üí° Tip: Use previews as a guide for framing and composition, not as expectations for your raw captures!
